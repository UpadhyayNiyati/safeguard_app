Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is Safety Assistant free?

Yes! Safety Assistant is completely free to use. We believe everyone deserves access to emergency services information.

❓ How do I add emergency contacts?

Log in to your account and navigate to "Emergency Contacts" from the top menu. Click "Add New Contact" and fill in the details. You can add as many contacts as needed.

❓ Is my location data safe?

Yes, your location is only used to find nearby emergency services. We never store or share your location data without your permission. Your privacy is our priority.

❓ What if I don't have internet access?

For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 directly. They don't require internet access and can provide immediate assistance.

❓ Can I use this outside my country?

Yes! Safety Assistant works worldwide. You'll get access to emergency services in your current location. Check local emergency numbers for best results.

❓ How accurate are the emergency service locations?

We use OpenStreetMap data which is regularly updated. However, always verify details by calling ahead to confirm current operating hours and services.

Safety Tips

✓ Know Emergency Numbers

Memorize important emergency numbers for your country. Safety Assistant can help, but knowing them is crucial.

✓ Update Contacts Regularly

Keep your emergency contacts updated with current phone numbers and relationships.

✓ Share Your Contacts

Let your emergency contacts know they're on your list so they can help if needed.

✓ Stay Calm

In emergencies, stay as calm as possible and provide clear information to responders.

✓ Trust Your Instincts

If something feels unsafe, don't hesitate to reach out for help or call emergency services.

✓ Test Locations

Verify emergency service locations aren't too far from your usual areas.

Still Need Help?

Our support team is here for you. Don't hesitate to reach out.
