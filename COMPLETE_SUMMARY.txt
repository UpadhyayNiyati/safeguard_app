═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  ⚠️  IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SafeGuard is a SUPPLEMENTAL TOOL for emergencies.

IN LIFE-THREATENING SITUATIONS:
  1. CALL 911 IMMEDIATELY (or local emergency number)
  2. Do NOT rely solely on this app
  3. Do NOT wait for map or location
  4. Contact emergency services FIRST
  5. Use SafeGuard as additional help

SafeGuard cannot:
  ✗ Replace official emergency services
  ✗ Guarantee response times
  ✗ Provide 100% location accuracy
  ✗ Work as primary emergency tool

SafeGuard can:
  ✓ Help find emergency services
  ✓ Provide quick reference info
  ✓ Organize emergency contacts
  ✓ Supplement emergency protocols
